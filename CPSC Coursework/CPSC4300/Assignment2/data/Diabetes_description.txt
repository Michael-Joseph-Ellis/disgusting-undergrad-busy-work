This dataset is originally from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases. The objective of the dataset is to diagnostically predict whether or not a patient has diabetes, based on certain diagnostic measurements included in the dataset. Several constraints were placed on the selection of these instances from a larger database. There are in total 768 instances and 8 features. In particular, all patients here are females at least 21 years old of Pima Indian heritage.


Pregnancies: Number of pregnancies
Glucose:Glucose level in blood
BloodPressure: Blood pressure measurement
SkinThickness: Thickness of the skin
Insulin: Insulin level in blood
BMI: Body mass index
DiabetesPedigreeFunction: Diabetes percentage
Age: Age
Outcome: Final result (1: Yes, the individual has diabetes; 0: No, the individual does not have diabetes)
